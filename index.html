How to become healthy?

Yes, You can also become healthy.
What you need is just to follow the following steps consistently with determination and a positive thinking.
1

Determination

Firmly determine your health goal and keep on that. Your health is in your hands only.
2

Proper Diet

Have proper diet that suits your body nature and helps you to stay healthy.
3

Exercise/Yoga

Physical exercises like Yoga are basic and most important step to become fit.
4

Meditaion

Health is not only about physical fitness. Meditaion is the best mental medicine.
5

Stay Happy

Everything is worthless unless you are happy. Stay happy and enjoy your life.
